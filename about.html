About Us

Welcome to United Homecare and Nurse Staffing LLC

At United Homecare and Nurse Staffing LLC we have proudly dedicated the last 9 years to providing compasionate and comprehensice care for our seniors. Our mission is to ensure your loved ones feel comfortable and supported in their daily lives. Our team of skilled caregivers offer a wide range of services tailored to the unique needs of each individual. From personal care such as bathing and grooming to essential mobility support and transportation, we ensure that every client receives the highest level of attention and assistance. Our commitment extends to housekeeping, food preparation and medication management, as well as fostering social engagement and advocay in medical and legal settings. At United HomeCare and Nurse Staffing LLC, we focus on creating a safe, comfortable, and supportive home enviroment, wher each person is treated with the respect and dignity they desreve.
